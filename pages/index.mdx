## What We Build

JStility creates open-source developer tools that are:

- **Smart by default** — work out of the box with sensible defaults, no configuration required to get started
- **Flexible** — easily configurable when you need more power, with plugins and custom hooks
- **Respectful** — built on existing standards and APIs, integrating seamlessly with your current workflow
- **Community-driven** — transparent development, welcoming contributions from everyone

Every tool we publish follows these principles. We believe great tools should be invisible until you need them.

## Our Principles

1. **Simplicity first** — the best tool is the one you don't notice. We start with zero configuration and let you opt into complexity.
2. **Respect the ecosystem** — we build on top of battle-tested libraries like the TypeScript compiler API, not against them.
3. **Open by default** — source code, roadmap, and discussions are all in the open.
4. **Inclusive** — contributions of all kinds are valued, whether it's code, documentation, design, or feedback.

## Join the Community

All our projects are open source and hosted on [GitHub](https://github.com/jstility).  
Here's how you can get involved:

- **Contribute code** — pick an issue tagged `good first issue` and submit a pull request
- **Improve documentation** — help us keep our docs clear, accurate, and beginner-friendly
- **Report bugs** — open an issue with a clear reproduction if something doesn't work as expected
- **Suggest features** — share your ideas for making our tools even better
- **Spread the word** — star our repos, share with colleagues, or write about your experience

We're always happy to help new contributors get started. Reach out on GitHub or open a discussion.
