# Support JStility

If our tools have helped you or your team, consider supporting their development.  
Every contribution helps us maintain, improve, and create new open-source tools for the community.

## Why Donate?

- **Sustainability** – funding keeps our projects actively maintained and up-to-date
- **New Features** – your support accelerates development of long-requested capabilities
- **Infrastructure** – covers hosting, CI/CD, and other operational costs
- **Independence** – allows us to stay community-focused without corporate constraints

## Other Ways to Support

If financial support isn't possible right now, there are still many ways to help:

- **Star our repos** on GitHub to increase visibility
- **Share** our tools with your colleagues and network
- **Contribute** code, documentation, or bug reports
- **Provide feedback** on features and usability

Every bit of support counts. Thank you for being part of the journey!
